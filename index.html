Next text prediction

This app aims to predict the next word when you enter text. The model works depending on the type of situation. It allows you to predict according to what you write, a blog, a news, or a tweet. It predicts the 3 most popular words. The words appearing before the entry are the 3 most used words for each type of text. 

[1] "i"   "the" "and"
[1] "the" "i"   "he"
